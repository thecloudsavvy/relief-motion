FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer tele‑rehab?

Yes. Tele‑rehab is a core digital health product used for follow‑ups, adherence support, and continuity between in‑person sessions.

Do you work with hospitals and HMOs?

Yes. See the Partnerships portal for referral loops, reporting, and integration model.

How is pricing determined?

Pricing depends on diagnosis complexity, care model (in‑person vs hybrid), location, frequency, and required equipment/time. We provide transparent quotes before starting.
