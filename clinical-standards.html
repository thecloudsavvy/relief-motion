Our Clinical Standards

A partner‑ready operating standard for rehabilitation delivery—built for clinical integration and continuity of care.

Quality System

Designed for Clinical Confidence

Our standard ensures predictable delivery, measurable progress, and clinician‑friendly reporting—supporting hospitals, HMOs, and partner clinics.

01

Structured Assessment

Baseline measures and risk screening to support appropriate care plans.

  • Intake: diagnosis, acuity, co‑morbidities, precautions
  • Red‑flag screening and escalation triggers
  • Functional baseline measures and goals
02

Program Design

Phase‑based pathways aligned to condition type and clinical goals.

  • Neuro: 4‑phase protocol progression
  • Ortho: return‑to‑activity progression planning
  • Post‑op: precautions, staged loading, benchmarks
03

Clinical Documentation

Notes and summaries written for clinician-to-clinician clarity.

  • Session documentation (SOAP‑style)
  • Progress summaries and milestone tracking
  • Discharge summary with recommendations
04

Continuity & Follow‑Up

Hybrid care that supports adherence and reduces regression.

  • Tele‑rehab check‑ins between visits
  • Maintenance planning and adherence support
  • Clear handover to referring clinician
