FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start treatment?

In most cases, we can begin within 48–72 hours of your first contact. For urgent cases, we do our best to accommodate same-day or next-day visits.

Do I need any equipment at home?

No. We bring everything required — therapy bands, exercise balls, manual therapy tools, and all professional equipment. You just need comfortable clothing and a space approximately 3m × 2m.

How much does a session cost?

Sessions start from ₦25,000 depending on your condition, location, and the complexity of treatment required. We offer transparent, personalised quotes before you commit to anything.

How many sessions will I need?

This varies by condition. Acute back pain may resolve in 4–6 sessions. Stroke rehabilitation can be 2–3 sessions per week for several months. We'll give you a clear estimate after the initial assessment.

Is the physiotherapist fully licensed?

Yes. Our physiotherapist holds a B.Sc. in Physiotherapy and is fully registered with the Medical Rehabilitation Therapists Board of Nigeria (MRTM). We are happy to share our registration details on request.

Do you treat elderly patients?

Absolutely. Elderly care is one of our specialities. Our home-based approach is especially beneficial for older patients who find it difficult to travel to clinics. We use gentle, age-appropriate techniques throughout.
