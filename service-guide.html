Service Guide

A patient-friendly overview of what’s included, how programs are delivered, and how pricing is determined.

What’s Included

Program delivery

Relief Motion is program‑first. You receive an intake, baseline measures, a plan‑of‑care, and structured follow‑up. Programs can be delivered in‑person in Lagos and/or via tele‑rehab check‑ins.

• Clinical intake with screening, baseline measures, and goals.
• Program plan aligned to neuro, ortho, post‑op, or geriatric strategy.
• Progress reviews with milestone tracking and plan adjustments.
• Tele‑rehab follow‑ups to improve adherence between visits.
Pricing

How pricing is determined

We don’t use “one-size-fits-all” fees. Pricing depends on diagnosis complexity, care model (in‑person vs hybrid), location, visit duration, frequency, and equipment/time required. You’ll receive a clear quote before starting.

Note

If your case is referred by a hospital/clinic, our reporting loop and coordination are included as part of program delivery.
