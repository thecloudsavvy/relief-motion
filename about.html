About Relief Motion

Relief Motion is a physiotherapy and rehabilitation brand focused on making quality recovery care effective, accessible, and easier to begin across Lagos. We combine home physiotherapy, online consultation, structured programs, and partner-ready clinical pathways.

Home > About
Our Vision

A preferred recovery partner for patients, hospitals, and care teams.

We exist to help people move better after pain, surgery, stroke, injury, or loss of confidence. Our work is built around practical access: the patient should be able to receive expert assessment, a clear plan, and consistent follow-through without unnecessary friction.

Whether care happens at home, online, or through a partner facility, Relief Motion keeps the process structured, measurable, and personal.

Our Approach

Recovery works best when the patient understands the condition, the goal, and the actions required. We assess current function, explain what is happening, agree on realistic milestones, and guide patients through treatment that fits daily life.

Patients vouch for it too

Relief Motion patients are building confidence, strength, and independence through guided rehab and consistent clinical support.

4 Step Standardized Relief Motion Process

Every plan is shaped around understanding, recovery, maintenance, and prevention, so progress is not left to guesswork.

Maintenance

Awareness & Goal Setting

Recovery
