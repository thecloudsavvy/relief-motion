About the Practitioner

PT, Abatan T.D,
B.Sc. Physiotherapy

With over 8 years of clinical physiotherapy experience, I founded Relief Motion out of a deep belief: that every patient deserves expert care — regardless of whether they can travel to a clinic.

After working in leading hospitals across Lagos, I made the shift to home-based care, and the results spoke for themselves. My patients recover faster, feel more comfortable, and achieve outcomes that often exceed what's possible in a traditional clinic setting.

Registered with the Medical Rehabilitation Therapists Board of Nigeria (MRTM)
B.Sc. Physiotherapy — University of Lagos
Postgraduate training in Neurological Rehabilitation
8+ years clinical experience across Lagos hospitals
Specialist in stroke rehab, orthopaedic, and geriatric care
